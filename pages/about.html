About

The Minimalist Bible was created for people that want a cleaner and neater way to read the Bible without having to sort through menus, sign up for accounts or be bothered by annoying adds. The Minmalist Bible will always be simple and free to use.

Thank you for visiting this website. If you enjoy your experience of reading the Bible here, please share this website with others to help get the word out. Our best asset is you.
